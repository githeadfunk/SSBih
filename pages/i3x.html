SpectraMax i3x Multi-Mode Detection Platform

SpectraMax® i3x Multi-Mode Microplate Reader is a flexible, user-upgradeable microplate detection system with up to 8 modes of detection and unlimited application possibilities. Supported by industry-recognized SoftMax® Pro Microplate Data Acquisition and Analysis Software, users are now able to extend the ease-of-use of their typical plate reader applications to cell-based imaging and western blot detection.

Applications:

  • Measures spectral-based Absorbance, Fluorescence, and Luminescence
  • Increased fluorescence performance
  • Quantitative low-light measurement

Specifications

  • Plate formats:
    6 to 1536 wells
  • Light source:
    Spectral Fusion Illumination (Xenon flash lamp + high-powered LEDs or laser diode in detection cartridges)
  • Temp. control:
    4°C above ambient to 45°C
  • Absorbance photometric performance:
    Wavelength range: 230–1000 nm
    Wavelength bandwidth: 4.0 nm
    Photometric range: 0–4.0 OD
    Photometric resolution: 0.001 OD
  • Fluorescence intensity performance:
    Wavelength range: 250–850 nm
    Wavelength selection: 1.0 nm increments
    Bandwidth (EX/EM): Adjustable EX 9/15 nm EM15/25 nm
    Dynamic range: > 6 logs
  • Luminescence performance:
    Wavelength range: 300–850 nm
    Wavelength selection: Choice of simultaneous detection of all wavelengths or selection in 1.0 nm increments
    Dynamic range: > 6 decades
    Cross-talk: < 0.4% in white 96- and 384-well microplates
