Our Facility

The SSBIH is designed to integrate our strength in pathway design, plant and microbial engineering, shot-gun proteomics, bioinformatics, and mathematical modeling into a global-leading facility in synthetic and systems biology.

The emphasis is on synthetic biology, where three functional cores are integrated to enable a highly effective synthetic biology facility and enablement

  • Biodesign, optimization and control of gene/operon expression
  • Synthesis, large components and synthetic genome are assembled
  • Implementation, perform high-throughput functional characterization of the designed organisms
